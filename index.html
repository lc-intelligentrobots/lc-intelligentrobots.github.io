Learning & Control Lab

We develop reinforcement learning and optimal control methods with theoretical guarantees — stability, robustness, and performance — and deploy them on physical systems such as legged robots and autonomous vehicles.

About the Lab

Our mission is reliable autonomy in the real world. We build controllers and learning algorithms that keep robots stable, safe, and high-performing even under uncertainty, time delay, and disturbances.
